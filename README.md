## üìä Project Overview
This interactive Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of the macroeconomic and fiscal health of 217 countries. It visualizes global economic trends, evaluates fiscal risks, and compares performance across different income groups and regions.

> "When analyzing the economic indicators of the world's countries, we gain the opportunity to understand not only the financial status of individual nations but also global economic trends, risks, and opportunities." ‚Äî *Tyler Cowen*

## üöÄ Key Features
- **Dynamic Navigation:** Custom sidebar for seamless transitions between pages.
- **Economic Risk Scoring:** Weighted model combining Inflation, Debt, Unemployment, and GDP growth.
- **Geospatial Insights:** Interactive map integration using Microsoft Azure Maps.
- **Trend Analysis:** Historical tracking (2010‚Äì2024) of Total GDP, Real Interest Rates, and Government Revenues.
- **Advanced Filtering:** Drill down by Region, Sub-region, and Income Group.

## üìÅ Dashboard Structure
1. **General Economic Overview:** KPIs including Global GDP ($1.16E+15$), Average Inflation ($6.31\%$), Average Unemployment ($7.91\%$), Average Public Debt ($61.82\%$).
2. **Macroeconomic Indicators:** GDP vs interest rates, decomposition tree by region & income group.
3. **Fiscal Indicators:** Budget Balances, Government Revenues ($233.09T$) & Expenditures ($250.77T$).
4. **Conclusion & Forecast:** Risk-based segmentation; 54.84% of countries are "Low Risk", while lower-income nations face significant fiscal pressures.

## üìà Key Insights
- **Growth vs. Risk:** Economic growth continues in many regions, but high inflation and rising debt remain key threats.
- **Fiscal Resilience:** High-income nations show more resilience; lower-income nations face budgetary pressures.
- **Risk Distribution:** 41.47% of countries fall into "Medium Risk," requiring careful debt-to-GDP monitoring.
